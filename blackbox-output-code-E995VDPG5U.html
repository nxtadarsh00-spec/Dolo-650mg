Dolo 650

Paracetamol 650mg - Pain Relief & Fever Reducer

📋 Medicine Information

Generic Name: Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)

Brand Name: Dolo 650

Strength: 650 mg per tablet

Form: Tablet

Uses:

  • Relief from mild to moderate pain (headache, toothache, muscle pain, backache)
  • Reduces fever
  • Common cold and flu symptoms
  • Post-surgical pain relief

💊 Dosage Guidelines

Age Group Recommended Dosage Frequency
Adults (13+ years) 1-2 tablets (650-1300mg) Every 6-8 hours
Children (6-12 years) 1/2 to 1 tablet (325-650mg) Every 6-8 hours
Maximum Daily Dose 4g (4000mg) Do not exceed

⚠️ Precautions & Warnings

Important Safety Information

Do not exceed recommended dosage. Overdose can cause severe liver damage.

Side Effects

  • Rare: Nausea, vomiting, stomach pain
  • Allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling)
  • Liver damage (with overdose)

Contraindications

  • Severe liver disease
  • Allergy to paracetamol
  • Do not use with other paracetamol-containing products

Drug Interactions

  • Warfarin (may increase bleeding risk)
  • Other pain relievers containing paracetamol
  • Alcohol (increases liver damage risk)

🩺 When to Consult a Doctor

  • Pain persists for more than 3 days
  • Fever lasts more than 3 days
  • Symptoms worsen or new symptoms appear
  • Signs of allergic reaction
  • History of liver/kidney disease
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding

⚕️ Always consult your healthcare professional before use.
